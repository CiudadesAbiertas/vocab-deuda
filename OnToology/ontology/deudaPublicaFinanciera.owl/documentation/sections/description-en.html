Vocabulary for the representation of the Financial Public Debt of a City Council: Description return to index

Figure 1 shows the classes and properties of the vocabulary that represents the Financial Public Debt as the main indicator of the public accounts of a city council:

In this vocabulary there is a class that represents the annual public financial debt [esdeufin:DeudaAnual](#DeudaAnual). The properties refer to the fiscal year and the total debt following the excessive deficit protocol (PDE). In this way, the debt of several fiscal years can be represented and thus the evolution of the debt of a city council may be known. The debt of a fiscal year is related to one or more financing instruments [esdeufin:InstrumentoFinanciacion](#InstrumentoFinanciacion) through the property ''financing instrument'' [esdeufin:instrumentoFinanciacion](#instrumentoFinanciacion). A financing instrument has properties such as the identifier, the interest type, the interest rate if the interest is fixed and the reference and margin if the interest is variable. A financing instrument may be a loan [esdeufin:Prestamo](#Prestamo) or an issuance [esdeufin:Emision](#Emision). A loan may be granted by one or more lender entities ([esdeufin:entidadPrestamista](#entidadPrestamista)), that are represented through the class [org:Organization](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-org/#org:Organization) from the ontology [Organization](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-org/). The loan may be of a certain type [esdeufin:tipoPrestamo](#tipoPrestamo) such as bilateral, sindicated, loan through a fund or a reimbursable aid. The loan type is represented with a SKOS taxonomy [tipo-prestamo](http://vocab.linkeddata.es/datosabiertos/kos/economia/deuda-financiera/tipo-prestamo). The lender entity may be of a certain type such as a financial entity, a deputation, etc. For this we have reused the property [org:classification(http://www.w3.org/ns/org#Organization) from the organization ontology which has as Range a SKOS taxonomy ''lending entity type'' [tipo-entidad-prestamista](http://vocab.linkeddata.es/datosabiertos/kos/economia/deuda-financiera/tipo-entidad-prestamista). A financing instrument is related through the property ''repayment schedule'' [espresup:planAmortizacion](#planAmortizacion) with the repayment calendar that indicates the date and amount of each repayment. A financing instrument has a living capital [esdeufin:CapitalVivo](#capitalVivo) that indicates at a certain date the amount to be repayed. Finally, each financing instrument may have a financial burden in a fiscal year, [esdeufin:CargaFinanciera](#CargaFinanciera), which may be a financial expense or a financial liability.

3.1 Examples back to indexe

In order to facilitate the comprehension of the use of some classes and properties of this vocabulary, some examples will be provided below. The first example refers to the annual debt of a city council. The fiscal year is 2019, and then the debt by the excessive deficit protocol (PDE) for that year is 2.232.568.750 euros. The debt that year corresponds to those financing instruments with living capital, in this case we show the relationship with a loan and an issuance which are detailed below.
