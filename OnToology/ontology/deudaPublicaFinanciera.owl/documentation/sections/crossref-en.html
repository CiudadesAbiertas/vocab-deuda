Cross reference for the Vocabulary for the representation of the information of the Financial Public Debt of a City Council: classes, object properties and data properties back to ToC

This section provides details for each class and property defined by the Vocabulary for the representation of the information of the Financial Public Debt, taking into account that the public debt is one of the main indicators of the public accounts and helps to understand the economic situation of a city council.

Organizationc back to ToC or Class ToC

Represents a collection of people organized together into a community or other social, commercial or political structure.
